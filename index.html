Use Cases

Key Applications

Use Case 1 Retail customer
Personal Banking
Retail customers assess potential energy efficiency upgrades for their home and use a Bank X loan or mortgage add-on to execute those upgrades. Lightspark provides the score, carbon, and energy story in plain language.
Use Case 2 Lending advisor
Mortgage Advisory
Bank X lending specialists review customer Home Energy Scores and risk profiles, then match them with upgrade bundles and lending products. The model surfaces the impact on GDS/TDS ratios and cash flow.
Use Case 3 Insurance customer
Insurance Risk
Insurance customers see how resilience upgrades reduce their climate risk scores for fire, flood, wind, and heat. Bank X can tie specific measures to discounts or improved policy terms.
Use Case 4 Underwriter
Insurance Underwriting
Underwriters use consistent 1‚Äì5 peril scores, exposure maps, and resilience measures to support coverage decisions, deductibles, and climate riders. Data rolls up to portfolio-level views.
Use Case 5 Capital markets
Green Bonds
Bank X can identify top-performing homes in the mortgage portfolio by energy and resilience performance as a basis for green or sustainability-linked bond issuance.
Use Case 6 ESG reporting
Financed Emissions
Estimate the carbon footprint of Bank X's real estate portfolio and track how financed upgrades reduce financed emissions (tCO‚ÇÇe) over time, supporting climate commitments and disclosure frameworks.
