Overview

Lightspark × TD Bank

Lightspark connects home energy and climate risk intelligence to TD’s personal banking, insurance, and climate reporting workflows. The same underlying model shows up in three different journeys: homeowner, mortgage specialist, and underwriter.

TD Use Cases
Use Case 1
Personal Banking
Retail customer
Retail customers assess potential energy efficiency upgrades for their home, and use a TD loan or mortgage add-on to execute those upgrades. Lightspark provides the score, carbon, and energy story in plain language.

Mortgage Broker
Mortgage specialist / broker

Insurance
Insurance customer
